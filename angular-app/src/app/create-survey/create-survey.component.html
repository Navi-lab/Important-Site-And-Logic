Design your Own Feedback Form
To create a new Feedback Form, choose one of the following options:
Build new feedback form
Get started with a blank page. You'll need to add questions by using the tools in the left sidebar to build your Feedback Form.
If you have questions ready to go and want to copy and paste them to your Feedback Form, select My questions are already written.
Copy past feedback forms
You can create a copy of a feedback form that's in your account. This way you don't have to start from scratch or change past feedback forms that have already collected responses.
The feedback questions, logic, theme, and other feedback form design settings are carried over to the copy, but you'll need to create a new collector to send your feedback form.
